About Us

Our Story

Company Structure

AqwaGel is a fully owned subsidiary of Artyhomes Pvt Ltd.

Artyhomes Pvt Ltd has been a prominent wallpaper manufacturer and interior design leader in Sri Lanka since 2002. With over two decades of experience in premium printing and design, Artyhomes expanded its vision into the educational sector through AqwaGel — bringing the same world-class printing technology to educational materials.

Printing Excellence

All posters are produced using Artyhomes' professional-grade digital printing technology. This ensures vibrant, fade-resistant colors, razor-sharp text, and exceptional durability.

Educational Impact

Our posters are designed in collaboration with educators to maximize learning outcomes. From early alphabet recognition to complex world geography.

Bilingual Design

We proudly offer materials in both English and Sinhala, supporting Sri Lanka's bilingual education policy and ensuring accessibility for students across the country.

Island Wide Delivery

Based in Battaramulla, we deliver our educational products to schools, tuition centers, and homes across all 25 districts of Sri Lanka.

Our Mission

To make high-quality educational visuals accessible and affordable for every student in Sri Lanka, leveraging our parent company's 22+ years of printing excellence to transform learning spaces into inspiring environments.
